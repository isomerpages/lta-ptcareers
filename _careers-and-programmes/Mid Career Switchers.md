<h3>Career Conversion Programme (CCP)</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/careers-page-447x271-mid-career-switcher.jpg">
</div>
<p>Career Conversion Programmes (CCPs) help mid-career individuals undergo
skills conversion to embark on careers in the public transport industry.
Examples of CCPs in the public transport sector include those for Bus Technicians,
Station Managers, Assistant Station Managers, as well as Customer Service
Officers.</p>
